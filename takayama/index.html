How to Travel to Takayama

Takayama is located in the middle of Japan. The map above shows where it is. Below are instructions on how to get there.

    Get to tokyo
  • Go to Southwest.com.
  • Buy tickets from your location to Tokyo.
    Get to tokyo
  • Once you are in Tokyo there are 3 ways to get to Takayama:
    • The train which takes 4h 43m, and costs $120, or ¥18000
    • The bus which takes 6h 55m, and costs $50 or ¥7500

Takayama is a city in Japan. The language there is Japanese. Most people there don't speak English so if you are planning to go there it is best to know Japanese.

Fun Facts!
